Baking Buddies

Like to cook but don't want the mess?
Here, you can make a mess and we deal with the stress!

These sessions are a great opportunity for you to bond with your child in a fun kitchen environment where you don’t have to worry about the mess. We do all the cleaning up afterwards!

In these sessions, we focus on fun and creating something delicious to enjoy together. This could be anything from crumbles and cakes, to pizzas and pasta pots.

We might also fit in a sing song or a story if there’s time!
