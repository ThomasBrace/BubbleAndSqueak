Parties

Are you looking for a memorable party for your budding baker? A cookery party is something just a little bit different. You choose what you’d like to bake and I bring all the equipment and ingredients. The children receive a recipe booklet, recipe cards and a certificate. Depending on what the children are baking, they will also have a cupcake or pizza box to decorate and take their goodies home on (if there’s any left!).

We have a range of different packages on offer or you can contact me if you have something specific in mind.

For the afternoon tea party - a cake stand and afternoon tea style teacups will also be provided for the party.

2-hour* Afternoon Tea Party

(recommended age 7-12)

Activity 1 - Bigger Bake - Choose from one of the following:

  • Scones (fruit or plain)**
  • Mini Victoria sponge
  • Mini chocolate sponge
  • Mini lemon drizzle cakes

**Jam and cream provided for eating with scones once baked.

Activity 2 - Something Simple - Finger sandwiches / shapedsandwiches:

  • Cheese
  • Ham
  • Cream cheese and cucumber
  • Jam
  • Egg Mayonnaise

Activity 3 - Afternoon Tea is served

Children enjoy their afternoon tea, served in teacups, on cakestands and with pretty tea party plates.

They will be provided with cake boxes to take any leftovershome in, which they can decorate if time allows once they havefinished their afternoon tea.
